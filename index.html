Gameplay Mechanics

The Art of Visual Cooking

BistroX relies entirely on your choices. Select images from your pantry and combine them to reveal the final dish.

Choose Ingredients

Pick from a visual pantry of image-based items, like fresh onions, specific oils, and raw meats.

Combine & Method

Follow real cooking theories. Mix the specific ingredient images together to create your recipe base.

See the Result

Your final dish image is generated exclusively based on the exact combination you cooked up.
